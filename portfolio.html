Our Investment Philosophy

💡

Innovation First

We invest in companies solving real problems with creative solutions, even if those solutions seem crazy at first.

🚀

Growth Potential

We look for businesses that can scale rapidly and disrupt established markets. Sometimes we're right, sometimes... well, see PetCoin.

👥

Great Teams

We bet on exceptional founders who can execute their vision, adapt to challenges, and build strong company cultures.

🎯

Long-term Focus

We're patient investors who understand that building great companies takes time. Our average hold period is 5-7 years.
