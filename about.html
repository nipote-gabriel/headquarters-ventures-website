About Headquarters Ventures

Geniuses doing business.

Our Mission

At Headquarters Ventures, we cut through the noise and call bullshit on business. Too many people are getting rich selling snake oil to entrepreneurs and investors who don't know any better. We're here to arm you with real insights, brutal honesty, and the occasional laugh at the expense of those who take themselves too seriously.

Our Approach

We combine deep business experience with irreverent humor to deliver content that's both educational and entertaining. Whether we're dissecting the latest startup failures, analyzing market trends, or sharing war stories from the investment trenches, we promise to tell you the truth - even when it hurts.

In Competence We Trust

Our weekly newsletter is where the real magic happens. It's 5 minutes of pure, unfiltered business intelligence that will make you the smartest (and probably most cynical) person in any room. Fair warning: side effects may include an inability to tolerate corporate buzzwords and an overwhelming urge to call out obvious BS.
